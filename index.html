Who we are

Wood is a craft, not a shortcut

Tito Santur King Construction is a family-run contractor based in Totowa, NJ, specialized in log, timber and country homes. The owner is on every job — from the first walkthrough to the final coat of stain — so the person quoting your home is the one building it.

We explain every step in plain language — English or Spanish — so you always know what's happening, what it costs, and when it's done. Clean sites, honest timelines, and finishes we're proud to put our name on.
